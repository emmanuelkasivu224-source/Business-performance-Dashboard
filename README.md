# 📊 Sales & Profit Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive Sales & Profit Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using PivotTables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, and data visualization techniques.

It is designed to help business managers monitor sales performance, profitability, customer trends, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

## Project Objectives

- Analyze total sales and total profit.
- Track yearly profit performance.
- Compare sales across product categories.
- Monitor customer growth over time.
- Analyze sales by state.
- Identify seasonal sales trends.
- Identify the top five most profitable customers.

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales
- Total Profit

### Profit by Year
Visualizes yearly profit trends.

### Sales by Category
Shows how sales are distributed across product categories.

### Customer Count by Year
Displays customer growth over time.

### Sales by State
Highlights regional sales performance using a map.

### Monthly Sales Trend
Shows monthly sales patterns and seasonality.

### Top 5 Customers
Identifies the customers contributing the highest profits.

Some insights generated from the dashboard include:
